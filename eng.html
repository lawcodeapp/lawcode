About Our App

We focus on building mobile applications for Constitution of Kingdom of Cambodia, Criminal Code, Criminal Procedure Code, Civil Code, Civil Procedure Code and more. Our Goal is to make all law code accessible to all Cambodian and be easily use by everyone.

Law Code

We understand the importance of law codes to everyone especially people who practice law and law students. By having law code as a mobile application, We bring the value of ease of accessibility to everyone at anywhere and anytime.

Articles

Reading through hundreds of articles in law code to find a specific article can be really difficult and time consuming. By displaying all articles in a list, We make sure it becomes easier to scroll through all the article in the law code to find any specific article.

English Translation

In today's world, It is necessary to understand law code in multiple language as we engage in an interconnected world. By giving option to switch language, We deliver article in multiple language in a blink of an eye for user to achieve their work quickly with trustworthy quality.

Search

We always had in mind to focus on maximize the efficiency and effectiveness for your experience. By providing search function, You can find any specific article based on article's name, article's chapter name and article's definition instantly.

Zoom

We make sure that articles can be easily read by regardless of any age group. By including the zoom function, You can zoom in or zoom out the content to your need.

Share

As We work or study or know about article in law code, We definitely need a quick solution to share the article with our co-worker, classmate or friends. By supporting the share function, You can share any article across all platform instantly without any hustle.
